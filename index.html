Haicheng Liao

Biography: I'm currently a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City (SKL-IOTSC), University of Macau under the supervision of Prof. Zhenning Li and Prof. Chengzhong Xu. Before joining UM, I received my B.S. degree from UESTC in 2022. I am broadly interested in Intelligent Transportation Systerm, particularly in the area of Autonomous Driving. My current research interests includes: Trajectory Prediction, Accident Anticipation, and Visual Grounding for Autonomous Vehicles.
