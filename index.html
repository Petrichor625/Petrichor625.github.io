About Me


We are constantly looking for self-motivated RAs (with Funding) to join HUMAN LAB! If interested, please don't hesitate to email me!

I am currently a Ph.D. student supervised by Prof. Zhenning Li and Prof. Chengzhong Xu (IEEE Fellow). I am affiliated with the State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City (SKL-IOTSC) and the Department of Computer Science at the University of Macau.

Before joining UM, I received my B.E. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), China, in 2022.

I am broadly interested in Intelligent Transportation Systerm. My current research interests focus on Autonomous Driving and Transportation Safety. My research encompasses areas such as trajectory prediction, accident anticipation, and visual grounding for autonomous vehicles. On these topics, I have published more than twenty papers as the first or co-first author in prestigious journals such as IEEE TPAMI, COMMTR, IEEE TIV, Information Fusion, and AAP, as well as at leading AI conferences like AAAI, ACM MM, IJCAI, ECAI, and ICRA.

Over the years, I have had the honor of mentoring over ten outstanding students, many of whom have advanced to top-tier institutions like Tsinghua University, Peking University, Zhejiang University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, the University of Science and Technology of China, and Tongji University.

If you are passionate about research and innovation, I warmly invite you to join us on this exciting academic journey!
